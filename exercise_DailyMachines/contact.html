Surrender to vishnu - the easiest way to cross the ocean of life & death

  • vishnu is pre-mordial being and ocean of mercy
  • has emphasised in vedas , puranas and bhagavat gita that surrender to him is the easiest way of reaching him
  • he prevades the entire universe , both inside and outside
  • after doing surrender (sharanagati) , one should devote to him lovingly by
    1. nama sankirtana
    2. worshipping his archa roopa
    3. doing the obligatory duties for gods , ancestors , humans and other living beings - if we decide to live life of a householder
